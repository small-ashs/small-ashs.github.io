FEATURED WORKS

Discover Authentic Uyghur Cuisine in the Heart of the UK

Welcome to our cozy restaurant, where we bring the vibrant flavors of Xinjiang’s Uyghur heritage straight to the UK. Our dishes celebrate fresh, high-quality ingredients – tender halal lamb and beef, hand-pulled noodles, aromatic rice pilafs, and seasonal vegetables – all infused with signature spices like cumin, chili, and garlic. From hearty Uyghur pilaf and spicy big plate chicken to cumin-grilled kebabs and comforting soups, every bite tells a story of Central Asian tradition, Silk Road influences, and bold, natural tastes. Whether you’re craving a flavorful family meal or a taste of something truly unique, we invite you to experience the warmth and authenticity of Uyghur cooking right here in Britain.
Halal-certified and made with passion – come join us for a journey through taste and culture.
